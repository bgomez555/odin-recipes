Crockpot Italian Chicken

An easy, three-ingredient weekday meal

While this meal is presented as a crockpot dish, it can be easily cooked in a pan or other vessel.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) bottle Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Stir Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  3. Place chicken in a slow cooker, and pour dressing mixture over chicken. Cover and cook on low until chicken is tender, no longer pink in the center, and the juices run clear, about 8 hours.
